In a small town called Blacksburg far far away...

Allie Howe
Software Engineer


Allie grew up in a small homogenous suburb east of Cincinnati, Ohio. The bubbled, isolated community felt a lot like growing up on Tatooine. In pursit of diversity and an engineering education, Allie moved to Blacksburg and enrolled in Virginia Tech's College of Engineering in 2014. There she found her best friends as a little sister of Delta Phi Sigma, joined the Apex Center for Innovation and Entreprenuership as a student leader, and carried out Ut Prosim as a member of Hokie Nation.

Allie originially declared Mechanical Engineering but after witnessing the technologicial revolution going on around her she knew she had to join it. Anxious to contribute, Allie switched to Computer Science and remained on course to gradutate in May 2018. Daily engineers are making history. Stories will be told- and Allie is determined to be apart of the narrative.

After interning with GE Aviation, Allie went on to intern with VeloCloud in Mountian View, CA. There she worked with the most entertaining, enabling and hard-working team. Always looking for new opprotunties to learn more and further her programming skills, her story goes on.
